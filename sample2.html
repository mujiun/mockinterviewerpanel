What I Learned

Key takeaways from designing Reserve Now, Pay Later

  • Follow global trends — BNPL was already reshaping e-commerce; applying it to travel meant meeting users where their expectations already were
  • Survey early to validate fast — a quick survey with 1,000+ users confirmed demand before investing in design, saving weeks of speculative work
  • Flexibility is the unlock — reducing upfront commitment anxiety drove adoption more than any UI optimization could
  • Design for the ecosystem — balancing guest flexibility with host protection required thinking beyond a single flow
