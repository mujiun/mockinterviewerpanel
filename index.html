User Problems

Recruiters are overwhelmed by mass applications, they want to quickly filter the right candidates for interviews

So how do you make your resume stand out and get the green light from recruiters?

Resume Tip #1

Build your resume with ATS-friendly format

build it with Google Docs, scan resume with free ATS tool

Resume Tip #2

Tailor resume (experience, subtitle) to increase relevance

mention industry or type of products you worked on

Resume Tip #3

Use AI tools to refine your wording, cover all relevant skills

UX skills: design / collaboration / leadership / problem-solving

AI Prompt

Rewrite my resume for UX/Product Designer roles following these guidance:

• Write ATS-friendly bullets for Design / Collaboration / Leadership / Problem-Solving.
• Structure each bullet as: Action + Scope + Method + Impact.
• Keep each bullet to 1–2 lines whenever possible (max 3 lines).
• Use strong verbs, no fluff, consistent tense, avoid internal jargon
• Ensure the writing aligns with what most companies look for in UX/Product Designer roles

Example Output

• Shaped product strategy and defined end-to-end flows for XXX tool, reduced 60% user clicks on XX setup and improved adoption.

Portfolio Project Ideas

Some portfolio project ideas that let you demonstrate the right skills and increase relevance to your target role
Cover 2 types of projects to demonstrate the needed skillset and scope

0-1 Project

  • team work, bigger scope
  • talk more about how you designed the homepage and core user flows
  • show how you considered scalability and MVP tradeoffs

skills: collaboration, problem-solving

Growth / Redesign Project

  • can be done as a solo project
  • new feature / redesign for existing product
  • talk more about design exploration and how to use existing patterns to solve new problems

skills: design, problem-solving

Make your projects relevant to your target role

0-1 Project

  • An AI writing assistant that helps non-native speakers draft professional emails
  • A conversational AI tutor that adapts lesson plans to each student’s learning pace

skills: collaboration, problem-solving

Growth / Redesign Project

  • Redesign ChatGPT’s conversation history to support better retrieval and organization
  • Add AI-powered smart replies to a customer support dashboard

skills: design, problem-solving

0-1 Project

  • A group trip planner that syncs itineraries, budgets, and votes across travelers
  • A local experience marketplace connecting travelers with neighborhood hosts

skills: collaboration, problem-solving

Growth / Redesign Project

  • Redesign Airbnb’s check-in flow to reduce host-guest friction
  • Add personalized trip recommendations to Google Travel based on past bookings

skills: design, problem-solving

0-1 Project

  • A Gemini-powered study companion inside Google Classroom for K-12 students
  • A collaborative whiteboard tool built into Google Meet for hybrid teams

skills: collaboration, problem-solving

Growth / Redesign Project

  • Redesign Google Maps navigation for cyclists with safety-first routing
